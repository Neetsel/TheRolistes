About Us

The Rolistes Podcast is your London-based tabletop RPG show, discussing everything: games, life in London, best spots and events, life as immigrants, food, music and other geek stuff.

I go by the alias of Kalum and I will be your host.

Among other random things this will include interviews of roleplayers based in London, to ask them about their journey to London, gaming groups, good times at the table and in London.
