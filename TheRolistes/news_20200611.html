Stay Safe Home With Free & Pay-What-You-Want Tabletop RPGs

11 June, 2020

It’s been heartwarming to see the tabletop RPG community react with online games and conventions to counter the social-distancing imposed on individuals and clubs.

It turns out tabletop RPG publishers are rising to the challenge as well through making a plethora of core books, bundles and TTRPG supplement free or pay-what-you-want.

Other tabletop RPG content might be brought forward as part of a fundraising effort.

I thought it might be a good idea to try to collate them in a blog post.

Feel free to let me know if some are missing or if an offer ends.

To be clear, some games below might have been available as pay-what-you-want before the current social-distancing but they are still great to check out.

“Achtung! Cthulhu – FATE Guide to the Secret War” by Modiphius

“The Dark Eye Core Rules” and “The Dark Eye Quickstart Rules” as well as two solo adventures “The Vampire of Havena” and “Conspiracy of Mages” by Ulisses Spiele

“Clumsy Heroic Adorable Anticapitalist Penguins VS Business Goose’s Ivory Tower“, “This Is A Sad Place, Where Ghosts Live” and all the one-page and short games by Ursidice

Still Cheap Honourable Mentions
